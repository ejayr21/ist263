THE INFLUENCER HUB

Who Are We

Founded in February 2022 by our CEO Ejay Ramos, The Influencer Hub is a hub that is meant to provide students all across the country with the opportunity to work with the most exclusive brands and companies. The Influencer Hub is based out of Syracuse, NY but have offices all across the United States.

What Do We Do

The Influencer Hub is the place to go for someone who is interested in working in influencer marketing, social media marketing, or even beicoming a social media manager. Our job is to connect you with marketing agencies, brands, companies, as well as recruiters to help you get the opportunity to work with various premier brands.

Our Work

We have worked with various marketing agencies such as Archrival, Youth Marketing Connection, Riddle & Bloom, HerCampus, and many other agencies to provide students all across the country with the opportunity to work with established brands. Some of our previous programs that we have offered include working with Amazon Prime Student, HBO Max, TikTok, Pepsi, Hollister Co., Madewell, and many many more. We are constantly adding new opportunities as they open up, to provide our influencers with more opportunities to expand their network and their experience.
